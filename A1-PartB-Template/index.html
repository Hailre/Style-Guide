Colours

Our primary palette is a split complementary colour scheme that pairs cool, calming blues with warm oranges for strong visual contrast. These colours are positioned opposite on the colour wheel, creating a hierarchy, where blue will be used as a backdrop to highlight vibrant orange call-to-action buttons and headers. In doing this, a feeling of balance and harmony across the website’s user interface will be made.

These colours will go on to form a brand identity for our Freelancer website, allowing us to maintain a consistent and strong visual identity for new users.

Similar to existing media related to freelance employment, this site will be using a sans-serif typeface. This font will be kept in lowercase for both headings and body text (excluding display headings) for increased legibility. This is ideal for Wavelength as it is designed to be accessible to a broad demographic, allowing for those with different language backgrounds or disabilities to use the site without issue.
